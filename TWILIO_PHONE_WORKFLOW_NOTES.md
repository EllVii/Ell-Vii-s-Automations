- "Unlimited calls and texts included."
- "Twilio is free."

## Three phone paths

### 1. Full CRM Phone Control
Port the business number into Twilio.

Best for:
- Full call/text control
- Call logging
- Missed-call capture
- Voicemail workflow
- SMS inside CRM
- Clean ELA automation
- Strong attribution

### 2. Low-Risk Forwarding
Keep the current carrier and forward calls to a CRM-connected Twilio number.

Best for:
- Testing the CRM phone workflow
- Avoiding porting disruption
- Nervous clients

Downside:
- Weaker SMS control
- Attribution may be less clean

### 3. Hosted SMS
Keep voice with the current provider and host SMS/MMS with Twilio when eligible.

Best for:
- Clients who want CRM texting but do not want to move voice service.
